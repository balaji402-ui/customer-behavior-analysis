# 🛒 Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

An end-to-end **Data Analytics Project** that analyzes customer shopping behavior using **Python**, **PostgreSQL**, **Power BI**, and **Gamma AI**. The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow—from data cleaning and exploratory analysis to SQL-based business insights, interactive dashboard development, and professional business reporting.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **3,900 customer purchase records** across multiple product categories to uncover customer spending patterns, purchasing behavior, subscription trends, and product preferences.

The project follows a real-world data analytics pipeline:

- 🐍 Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering using Python
- 🗄 Database Integration with PostgreSQL
- 💻 Business Analysis using SQL
- 📊 Interactive Dashboard Development in Power BI
- 🤖 Professional Presentation & Report Creation using Gamma AI

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer shopping behavior
- Understand customer demographics and spending patterns
- Identify high-value customers
- Compare subscriber vs non-subscriber performance

## 📈 Key Insights

- Male customers generated significantly higher total revenue than female customers.
- Clothing contributed the highest revenue among all product categories.
- Express shipping customers had a slightly higher average purchase amount.
- Loyal customers formed the largest customer segment.
- Young Adults contributed the highest overall revenue.
- Certain products depended heavily on discounts to drive sales.
- Subscription adoption remained relatively low despite many repeat buyers.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase subscription benefits to improve customer retention.
- Launch loyalty programs for repeat customers.
- Optimize discount strategies to maintain healthy profit margins.
- Promote top-rated and best-selling products through targeted campaigns.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue customer segments and shipping preferences.
